Glossary
Market Capitalization: The total value of issued shares of a publicly traded company. Equal to the current share price multiplied by the
number of shares outstanding. This represents the public consensus on the value of a company's equity.
Large-cap (capitalization): Short for 'large companies' with Market Capitalization > $10 billion.
Mid-cap: Medium-sized companies; Capitalization between $2 and $10 billion.
Small-cap: Small companies between $250 million and $2 billion Capitalization.
